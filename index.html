Welcome to MedVR

A free VR medical situation tester.

Our Objective

Knowing what to do in a situation is vital to human survival. We believe everyone should have the basic knowledge should the day come when they need it.

How It Works

A situation is presented to you and youre goal is to handle the situation with the persons health in mind. Everyday scenerios to extreme ones will be shown.

How We Teach

At the end of each scenerio you will see the correct response to each situation. A scoring scystem will soon be added.
